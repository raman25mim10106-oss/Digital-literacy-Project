##### Social Media Do’s #####

Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review and update your profile's privacy settings to control who can view your content.


Think Before You Post: Always consider if a post could negatively impact your future career or academic reputation.


Verify Information: Fact-check news or academic resources before sharing them to avoid spreading misinformation.


Network Professionally: Follow industry leaders and companies on professional platforms to stay informed about career opportunities.


Report Harassment: Use platform reporting tools immediately if you encounter cyberbullying or toxic behavior.


#### Social Media Don’ts ####

Don't Over-share: Avoid posting sensitive personal information such as your home address, phone number, or live location.


Don't Engage with Trolls: Avoid getting into heated, unprofessional arguments in public comment sections.


Don't Post Without Consent: Never upload photos or videos of classmates or professors without their explicit permission.


Don't Plagiarize Content: Avoid sharing someone else's work or creative designs without giving proper credit.


Don't Use Unprofessional Handles: Avoid using offensive or inappropriate usernames for accounts you intend to list on your resume.
